The Story

Born in 2001.
Reinvented
with AI.

PatientTrac began as a pioneering electronic medical record system — hand-coded in Visual Basic 6, running on Windows XP, serving plastic surgeons and nursing homes across the United States. It worked. It lasted. It held the clinical logic of thousands of patient encounters.

Now, that same clinical wisdom — the validation rules, the surgical workflows, the field-by-field knowledge of what a plastic surgeon truly needs — has been extracted, preserved, and rebuilt from the ground up with AI at its core.

2001
PatientTrac is born

Built in Visual Basic 6 on Windows XP. The first electronic medical record system purpose-built for nursing home and long-term care providers. Pioneering structured clinical data capture at a time when most practices still used paper charts.

Legacy Foundation
2005
Plastic Surgery module launched

Expanded into plastic and reconstructive surgery with dedicated modules for breast examination, OR scheduling, surgical proposals, and post-operative care. frmPlasticSurgery becomes the hub of an entire clinical workflow.

Specialty Expansion
2010
Clinical depth grows

Diagnosis ICD coding, mental status assessment, emergency contact management, and inpatient appointment scheduling added. The system accumulates over 40,000 lines of clinical business logic across 30+ forms.

Clinical Depth
2020
The reinvention begins

Decision made to preserve 20 years of clinical wisdom and rebuild on modern infrastructure. Every VB6 form analyzed. Every validation rule extracted. Every SQL query documented. Nothing lost — everything elevated.

Migration Begins
2026
Revela launches

PatientTrac Revela — the complete reinvention. AI-guided patient intake, real-time clinical flags, surgical proposal generation, and full practice operations. Built on React, TypeScript, Anthropic Claude, and Supabase. The same trusted clinical logic. Infinitely more powerful.
